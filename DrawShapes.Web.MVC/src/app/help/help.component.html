How To

Please enter a natural language command to draw the shape. Example: Draw a(n) [shape] with a(n) [measurement] of [amount] (and a(n) [measurement] of [amount]). Please also see examples below on how to draw a specific shape.

How to Draw a Circle

You can draw a circle with either mentioning radius, height or width. When you specify height or width, it draws the circle with half the radius of that height or width value.

Examples:
Draw a circle with a radius of 100.
Draw a circle with a height of 100.
Draw a circle with a width of 100.

How to Draw an Oval

You can draw an oval with mentioning both height and width, since oval has different height and width value.

Examples:
Draw an oval with a height of 100 and a width of 200.

How to Draw a Triangle

You can draw a triangle with mentioning either height or width or both height and width. When you mention both height and width (different values), it draws an isosceles triangle. When you mention just height or width, it draws a regular triangle with same sides.

Examples:
Draw a triangle with height of 100.
Draw a triangle with width of 200.
Draw a triangle with width of 200 and height of 100.

How to Draw a Square

You can draw a square with either mentioning height or width, because a square has same height and width.

Examples:
Draw a square with a height of 100.
Draw a square with a width of 100.

How to Draw a Rectangle or Parallelogram

You can draw a rectangle or parallelogram with mentioning both height and width which also differ in value, since both rectangle and parallelogram have different height and width dimensions.

Examples:
Draw a rectangle with a height of 100 and a width of 200.
Draw a parallelogram with a height of 150 and a width of 250.

How to Draw a Pentagon or Hexagon or Heptagon or Octagon

You can draw a pentagon or hexagon or heptagon or octagon by mentioning the length of its sides.

Examples:
Draw a pentagon with a side of 200.
Draw a hexagon with a side of 150.
Draw a heptagon with a side of 120.
Draw an octagon with a side of 100.
